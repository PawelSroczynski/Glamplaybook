## 1. Executive Summary

**GlampModeler Ultimate** is a browser-based financial simulation engine and operational command center for the Glamping industry.

### Product Vision (One Sentence)

> "A tool that answers: How much will my glamping site cost, what will I earn, and am I on track?"

### Target Users

| Persona | Primary Need | Key Feature |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| **Pre-Investment Owner** | "Should I build this?" | CapEx calculator + ROI projection |
| **Active Operator** | "Am I profitable?" | Daily dashboard + variance tracking |
| **Growth-Focused Owner** | "How do I grow?" | Marketing ROI + guest LTV |

### Success Metrics

| Metric | MVP Target | Full Product Target |
|--------|------------|---------------------|
| User completes projection | 80% completion rate | 90% |
| Time to first insight | < 10 minutes | < 5 minutes |
| Return usage (weekly) | N/A | 60% of operators |
